Salvation Bush Walk

View track route details

The Salvation bush walk is part of the Wrights Hill loops. It is the shorter of the two available tracks, taking about 45 minutes to complete.

This walk is nice and easygoing for all to enjoy.

Getting there:

Get the number 21 bus to Crodyn Street.

The bush walk entrance is a 20 minute walk from here, a great way to warm up for your stroll.
