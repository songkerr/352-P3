Mount Albert Loop

View track route details

The Mt Albert Loop is a 2km walk, taking about 90 minutes.

This loop walk has a common entry and exit point and is suitable for those with average fitness. The walk goes behind Wellington Zoo, giving views of Cook Strait. It also includes Four Cross Track - a specialised mountain bike downhill race track, suitable for those at an intermediate level.

Getting there:

Catch a number 23 bus to Sutherland Road at the corner of Sutherland Crescent, then walk to the start.
